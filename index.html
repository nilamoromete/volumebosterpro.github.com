Privacy Policy for Volume Booster Pro

Last updated: [Current Date]

Data Collection and Usage

Volume Booster Pro is designed with user privacy in mind. We are committed to being transparent about our data handling practices:

What We Access

  • Audio streams from browser tabs (required for volume enhancement and audio processing)
  • Local storage for saving user preferences (volume levels and equalizer settings)

How We Handle Your Data

  • All audio processing is performed locally in your browser
  • No audio content is recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • User preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • No personal information is collected or transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking mechanisms are implemented

Third-Party Services

The extension uses Font Awesome CDN solely for loading UI icons. No user data is shared with this or any other third-party service.

Data Storage

User preferences (volume levels, EQ settings, and theme preferences) are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension.

Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • tabCapture: Required to access and enhance audio streams
  • storage: Required to save your preferences
  • activeTab: Required to detect and process audio in the current tab
  • offscreen: Required for continuous audio processing
